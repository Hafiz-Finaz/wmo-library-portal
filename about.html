Our Legacy

Empowering Through Academic Excellence

WMO Imam Gazzali Academy was established to provide higher academic and religious learning. The Library Council plays a central role in offering digital learning opportunities to students, researchers, and community learners.

We leverage serverless infrastructure, digital book formats, reading progress trackers, and detailed reviews to make learning seamless and easily accessible for modern education.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about digital borrowing, shelf locations, or memberships? Find quick answers below.

How do I borrow a book?

Log in to your account, search for the book, and click the "Borrow" button. If the book is available, the request will be submitted to the librarian for approval. Once approved, you can read it online or collect the print edition from the designated shelf location.

Can I download E-Books directly?

Yes, books with uploaded PDF digital copies can be previewed using our built-in PDF Reader or downloaded for offline study. Look for the "Download" or "Preview PDF" buttons in the details popup.

How do I find print books in the library?

Every book record includes a "Shelf Location" attribute (e.g. Shelf B4, Almirah 2). You can locate the physical copies in the academy campus library using this index.
