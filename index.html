WMO Imam Gazzali Academy Library Council

The WMO Imam Gazzali Academy Library is the central academic library of WMO Imam Gazzali Academy, established to support students, teachers, researchers, and scholars by providing quality learning resources in both traditional and digital formats. The library serves as a knowledge center that promotes reading, research, and lifelong learning while preserving Islamic and general academic literature.
